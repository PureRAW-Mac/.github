## 🖌 Overview  
PureRAW Advanced is a specialized RAW image preprocessing application from DxO that applies the company's world-class DEEP PRIME noise reduction and optical lens corrections to camera RAW files before they enter your editing workflow, extracting the maximum possible quality from any capture.

DEEP PRIME noise reduction uses deep learning trained on millions of images to separate genuine photographic detail from noise with an accuracy that traditional denoising algorithms cannot approach, producing clean images that retain sharpness and texture at high ISO settings.

Lens correction profiles sourced from DxO's laboratory measurements apply precise optical corrections for distortion, vignetting, chromatic aberration, and sharpness uniformity calibrated for specific camera and lens combinations rather than generic corrections.

Workflow integration exports processed DNG files back to Lightroom, Capture One, or any other editor with the quality improvements embedded, allowing you to continue your normal editing process with better starting material.

For photographers who shoot in challenging light conditions or with wide-angle lenses that introduce optical distortions, PureRAW Advanced is the preprocessing step that elevates the quality ceiling of what their images can become through editing.
